Melia's Pasta Recipe

A pasta recipe that my friend Melia taught me how to make. I spin it based on whatever I have in the house

This one is dedicated to them! Hi Meli, watching Girls right now with Makasha.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Salt
  • Tomato Paste
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Butter
  • Pasta Shape of Your Choice

Steps

  1. Cook pasta shape on the side according to boxed instructions
  2. Heat the olive oil in the pan and add the onions then the garlic till fragrant
  3. Hey add a little salt why not
  4. Add the red pepper flakes and then the tomato paste and mix to evenly coat
  5. Once the tomato paste is cooked up a bit, add halved cherry tomatoes
  6. Hey add a little salt why not
  7. Cook down the cherry tomatoes and pop them a bit to your liking
  8. Add a little pasta water and then the butter
  9. Cook that down while you drain the pasta
  10. Toss pasta in the sauce
